Our Teaching Philosophy

Learn Today...Lead Tomorrow

Tiny Oaks International is inspired and follows the guidelines of renowned early child development experts like:

Multiple Intelligences

The Theory of Multiple Intelligences was developed in 1983 by Dr. Howard Gardner, professor of education at Harvard University.

Dr Gardner proposes 8 different intelligences to account for a broader range of human potential in children and adults.

Our teaching programs engage children in activities and extensions that emphasize higher level thinking skills, creative thinking, academic skill development and imagination to help children learn and grow in an educational, dynamic and nurturing environment.

Montessori education

Montessori education is an educational approach developed by Italian physician and educator Maria Montessori. Maria developed many of her ideas while working with children. Her approach is characterized by an emphasis on independence, freedom within limits and respect for a child’s natural psychological, physical and social development.

Phonics Reading Program

Tiny Oaks follows a well-defined structured reading programme using Phonics and Glenn Doman methodology.
